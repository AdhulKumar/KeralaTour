Thiruvananthapuram

Thiruvananthapuram, the capital city of Kerala, is a vibrant blend of tradition and modernity. It boasts an array of historical landmarks, serene beaches, and bustling markets. The city's cultural richness is evident in its temples, art galleries, and museums.
